Resilient Redis queue

The Job queue

  1. Because of the nature of the networks and technology used to create non-fungible tokens (NFTs), there are multiple potential points of failure.
  2. To account for this, a resilience work queue is employed, and each step is attempted until the NFT is successfully deployed. If a job fails after ten attempts, it is called unrecoverable.
